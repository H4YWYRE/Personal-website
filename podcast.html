APEX Podcast

APEX is a podcast I started to understand but also teach fellow CS students what it's like as a Computer Science student after graduation. Every episode, I talk about a topic, "How to do well in a technical interview", "Should I work at a startup?", "Will programming jobs be automated?" And many more.
At the end of each episode I sit down with someone who has expertise and knowledge in the subject and I ask them some questions.
So if you're someone who's begun as a CS major or about to graduate as one, then listen to me! (pun intended)

Have a listen!
